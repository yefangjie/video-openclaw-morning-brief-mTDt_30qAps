🤖 OpenClaw Morning Brief: The First Use Case You Need to Set Up

Just discovered an amazing way to start my day with OpenClaw!

The "Morning Brief" is a 2-minute automated message that gives you a complete outline for the day ahead:

📧 Email Summary
Scans all emails, identifies what needs replies, flags urgent messages

📅 Calendar Overview  
Lists all scheduled meetings with summaries

✅ Task Recommendations
Reviews task list and suggests high-priority items for today

📰 News Digest
Reads overnight news and filters relevant stories based on your industry (Tech & AI for me!)

The best part? It's completely automated and personalized.

Key Benefits:
⏱️ Saves time by automating information gathering
🎯 Ensures nothing important is missed
🔧 Provides personalized, industry-relevant content
📈 Helps prioritize daily activities
